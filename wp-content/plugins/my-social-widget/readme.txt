== Description ==

The Social Media Icons widget takes a simple, extendable approach to displaying links to your social media profiles in WordPress. 
The purpose of this plugin was to strip away the complexities I found most other plugins to have and simply display a set of basic social icons in an list. 
There's no frills and no fanciness, making it easy to style to your website's look.


Its Seo friendly and doesnot requires any other optimization

== Installation ==

Download the zip file and upload to your WordPress installation. Upon activation, widget is available under Appearance > Widgets. Drag the widget into your sidebar and populate the profiles you wish to show on your website.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Where can I find social Media Widget? =
 Go to **Appearance > Widgets** and use the Social Media Widget** widget that comes with the plugin.
= How to add the media icons =
 Just add the url of your favourite social media account and that's it.
